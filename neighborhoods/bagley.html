BAGLEY

The Bagley Community is bounded by West Outer Drive (north), McNichols (south), Livernois (east), and Wyoming (west). Providing information, support to block clubs is just one way we help sustain & improve the quality of life for our residents. With over 5,000 homes, we are a “bedroom” community in the City of Detroit with good schools, retail, recreation, transportation and diversity in houses of worship.
Nearby Neighborhoods: Blackstone Park, Fitzgerald, Greenwich, Harmony Village, Pembroke.
